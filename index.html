Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe!

how to make a simple Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Cheese slices (of your choice)
  • Butter or Mayo

Instructions

  1. Lay out 2 slices of Bread.
  2. Spread butter or mayo on the ouside of your bread.
  3. Put 2 slice of cheese on one slice of bread, one center with the bread, one diagnoal with the bread.
  4. Put the 2 slices together
  5. In a pan on medium heat, melt 1 tablespoon of butter.
  6. Cook until golden brown or for about 2 minutes
  7. Flip the bread on the other side and cook for 45 more seconds.
  8. Enjoy!
